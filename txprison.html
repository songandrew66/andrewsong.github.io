Texas Prison Museum Rebranding

Worked on rebranding the Texas Prison Museum. Redeveloped a logo identity and visual language of the museum. After developing its logo and visual language I applied it into different visual systems. The Texas Prison Museum features numerous exhibits about the history of the Texas Prison Museum.

I Redesigned the Texas Prison logo to have a prison-like feel. The Previous logo features a blue chain and ball chained on bold type. I wanted to implement jail bars into the logo and represent the jail bars into a 'T' symbol. The 'T' symbol also represents the first letter of the Texas Prison Museum. I reversed the 'T' and combined the two together in order to create a cage effect.

SnapGift Concept

SnapGift is an app concept that allows peer to peer transactions with gift cards. My inspiration for this concept came from the birthday party feature in Snapchatâ€™s geofilters. Snapchat gives you access to a one-day-only filter on your birthday. This led to a question: What if there is an additional feature that gives birthdays more meaning along with Birthday Snaps? A simple idea I came up with is implementing a gift card system in Snapchat that uses similar features like Snapcash.

How it Works

Snapgift is opened up by swiping right to Snapchat's messages and navigated through the bottom nav bar of the application. There you can see the Snapgift Icon placed in symmetry with the Snapcash Icon. Once you click on the icon a category of gift cards will slide up. Here you can choose which category suits your gift.

After you choose which giftcard you like a window will pop up that prompts you to enter your debit card information.

Once you enter your debit card information, you will be allowed to enter a set gift card amount. This page was inspired by Snapchat's "Enter CVV" warning for Snapcash.

You will then see a message of sending and receiving the gift card. After you click on the message it will show instruction and information on how to redeem your Snapgfit.

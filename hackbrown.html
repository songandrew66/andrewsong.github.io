Hack@Brown Web Design

At Hack@Brown this year I worked with two computer science engineers from Brown University on a web application. Brown University hosts many student-led workshops, but there is no way to keep track or be informed about these workshops. Our team decided to design a website that stores presentations, pdfs, links, videos, etc. A student visiting this website can search this database for previous workshops or update currently held workshops. When the students visit upon this workshop they can upload and download files to that workshop. I was able to collaborate with my team on its specifications and features and wireframe mockup version of the website.
